as a teenager whose streaming platforms were all set to kid-friendly, Just Add Magic was one of my favourite shows. it had just enough wondorous worldbuilding for me to actually enjoy the idea of cooking. also, i happened to watch it at the same time as some of my best friends. we fell in LOVE with that show.

so much so, in fact, that we made our own little cookbook inspired by the show. we would diligently meet up every weekend, search up a new recipe, make up some fancy sounding spice names and cook- and, of course, update the precious cookbook. it became a ritual, in fact.

going home after a year of college, i stumbled upon that cookbook- and it brought on a terrific wave of nostalgia. that was also when this task got assigned to me. as someone that doesnt like to sit and choose things very much, i decided my submission would be about what was on my mind the most at that point.

if you've watched the show too, take a trip down nostalgia lane with me. if you haven't watched the show, enjoy lore about a show you'll probably never watch. but you should. it's awesome.
