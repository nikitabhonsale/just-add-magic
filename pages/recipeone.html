recipe #1: chocolate cake

(or, as we called it, counterspell cake)

on this page:

ingredients how to make bonus: personal lore :D

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup oil
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 0.75 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 0.5 cup boiling water

Making

  1. Mix the ingredients: wet ingredients and dry separately.
  2. When adding the milk, add in parts: that is, add a bit and stir well, repeat til all the milk has been added.
  3. Add boiling water similarly as the milk. If you'd like, add some espresso powder.
  4. Mix the wet and dry ingredients
  5. Preheat the oven and oil/line the mold with baking paper
  6. Let the cake bake for 35 minutes
  7. Optional but highly recommended: add sprinkles :D

bonus: personal lore :D

every recipe, in the Just Add Magic world, has a riddle. the idea is that each recipe must have a "downside", some way to deter the protagonists from using the recipe unless it was absolutely necessary to.

we invented riddles for all our recipes. the one for this one read:

All you have to do is bake
this delicious chocolate cake
and any curse, you can break!
