recipe #2: cookies

(or, as we called it, cooking master choco-chip cookies)

on this page:

ingredients how to make bonus: personal lore :D

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 1.25 cup sugar
  • 1.5 cup butter
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • chocolate chips (per preference)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Making

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter and sugar.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Stir in the flour and baking soda until combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown.
