about the show

Just Add Magic is a family-friendly fantasy television series based on the book by Cindy Callaghan. The show follows three middle school friends—Kelly, Darbie, and Hannah—who discover an old magical cookbook in Kelly's attic. As they begin to try out its recipes, they realize each dish has a magical effect, often leading to mysterious or unintended consequences.

As the girls use the recipes to solve personal problems or help others, they uncover secrets about their town's history and the book’s previous protectors. Over time, the story expands to include new threats, enchanted curses, and deeper magical lore. The show blends mystery, friendship, and adventure, emphasizing teamwork and moral responsibility.

Originally released on Amazon Prime Video, Just Add Magic gained popularity for its wholesome storytelling, strong female leads, and engaging magical themes, appealing to both kids and families.
